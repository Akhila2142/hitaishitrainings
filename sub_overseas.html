Study in United States

Why Study in USA?

  • Home to 8 of top 10 global universities
  • Flexible education system with diverse programs
  • Strong research opportunities and funding
  • Optional Practical Training (OPT) for work after graduation

Admission Process

  • Standardized tests (SAT/ACT for UG, GRE/GMAT for PG)
  • Strong emphasis on extracurricular activities
  • Letters of recommendation required
  • Personal essays or statements of purpose

Visa Information

  • F-1 student visa required
  • Proof of financial support needed
  • SEVIS fee payment required
  • Visa interview at US embassy/consulate
